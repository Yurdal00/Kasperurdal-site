Crew profile.

Kasper Urdal is a gaffer based in Amsterdam. As head of the light department he translates the director's and DoP's vision into light — designing setups, running crews and keeping power flowing safely, from single-source scenes to arena-scale shows.

Recent credits include Dio, Antoon, Lil Kleine, Rolf Sanchez, Emma Heesters, Yves Berendse and Tino Martin, alongside commercial clients and the MUBI-distributed short film Het Komt Wel Goed.
